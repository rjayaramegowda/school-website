Sri Ramakrishna Vidyakendra School

To fulfill the educational needs of economically weaker sections who are deprived of quality education, Sangha started Ramakrishna Vidyakendra School in the year 1991 with a small number of students. The seed that was sown initially has grown tremendously and now has Pre- Nursery to Pre- University classes with more than 500 students.

School has three Units namely

  • Pre- School
  • Primary School
  • High School

Pre- School

Pre school education plays a vital role in a child’s life. The child is exposed here to the learning environment before formal schooling.

Pre – Nursery & Nursery are types of Pre- Schools. Our pre- School curriculum, Provides an array of activities which help the kids to learn different languags, communicative skill, discipline, good behavior, moral values etc., They also act as catalysts for an easier transition of a child from home to school environment.

Primary School

Primary education plays an important role in preparing children emotionally, mentally socially & Physically for future education & life long learning.

Primary section was started in the year 1991 & has classes 1st std to 7th std.

We have been restricting strength of each class between 30 & 40 to enable teachers to give individual attention for the all round development of each child

High School

The High school was started in the year 2001-02 & has been continuously getting an average of 97% result in the SSLC Board examination.

Year 2010-11 was a crown of our achievements as we got 100% result and our student Chaithra .M secured 5th rank in the state.

The school endeavours to develop the intellectual, aesthetic, moral, physical, Social and emotional potential of its students through innovative and inquiry based teaching & learning.

The growth of any educational institute depends on the quality of faculty & infrastructure. RKVK school is blessed with excellent infra structure and well qualified, experienced & committed faculty members who have great passion towards their profession.

To inculcate humility, discipline, obedience & other Indian cultural values in students unique functions – “ Mathru Vandana” and “ Raksha Bandhan” are conducted in which children worship their mothers by offering “Pada Puja”

Ramakrishna Seva Sangha

Ramakrishna Seva Sangha is the brain child of a team of likeminded philanthropists who are devotees of Shree Ramakrishna Ashrama Yadavagiri, Mysuru. Being ardent followers of the HolyTrio Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa, Matha Sharada Devi and Sri Swamy Vivekananda, their hearts went out to alleviate the miseries of the underprivileged and the down trodden.
