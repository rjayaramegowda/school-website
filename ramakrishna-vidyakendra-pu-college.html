Ramakrishna Vidyakendra PU College

Keeping in mind the pivotal nature of PU education and its role in determining the future and carrier goal of our young generation Sangha has established Ramakrishna Vidyakendra PU College in the year 2015-16 which started functioning from the academic year 2016-17.

We are following a Unique system of education which comprises of the following :

  • Class room teaching during regular hours
  • ‘Reading and doubt clearance classes’ as well as coaching classes for CET, JEE, NEET & other competitive exams after the class hours. 4.00 PM to 6.30 PM

    All under one roof & hence there is no need to go for tuition classes

  • Continuous assessment of students is made by conducting regular fortnightly & monthly tests.

    We believe that this type of education system helps to increase the strength of students mind expands the intellect, instill courage to face future challenges & to stand on ones own feet.

    To carry out all the responsibilities in a systematic manner. College section has a team of highly qualified, experienced dedicated teaching faculty.

  • Sending a messages to parents whenever their wards are absent to classes.

Agreement

Sangha has entered into an agreement with “Sri Pranamya Educational and Charitable Trust” to share responsibilities.

As per the agreement Sri Pranamya Educatinal and Charitable Trust is incharge of conducting coaching classes for CET, JEE, NEET & other competitive exams.

They also provide separate hostel facilities for Boys and Girls of our college students only & also transport facility for Hostel students.

In order to enforce discipline in the institution & monitor day to day activities we have a unit of dedicated members of the school committee, Heads of Primary, High School & College Sections and a committed well experienced Executive officer.

Facilities

  • Spacious class rooms with Glass Boards.
  • Ultra Modern “State of the Art”. Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Labs.
  • Well Maintained library cum reading room with news papers, magazines & about 6000 books.
  • A big spacious, separate “ Matha Sharada” auditorium with a seating capacity for about 1000 people & with a wide elevated platform.
  • Spacious play ground used to play both indoor & outdoor games like Volley Ball, Shuttle Cock, Ball Badminton, Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, Chess, Carrom, etc.,
  • 24 hours filtered drinking water facility.
  • Well maintained, hygiene separate was rooms for boys & girls.
  • 24 x 7 CCTV surveillance for security purpose.
  • Multimedia projector to show educational Videos.
  • Fee concession for students belongs to economically weaker sections.
  • Separate shed to park vehicles.

Activities

The institution has been encouraging co-curricular & extra curricular activities, listed below to nourish hidden talent of students.

  • Conducting Debate, Essay Writing, fancy dress & Music Competitions.
  • Conducting seminars & lectures by eminent resource persons on various subjects.
  • Conducting field trips & excursions to places of educational interest like Museums, Art Galleries, Zoos, Botanical Gardens etc.
  • Conducting Teachers Parents meeting to discuss about the performance of their wards & to get suggestions from parents for the improvement & academic standard.
  • Celebrating National & State festivals, arranging “ Rath Yatra” to celebrate Swamy Vivekananda Jayanthi
  • “Mathru Vandana “ programme is conducted every year to inculcate Indian culture in the minds of young generation.

Ramakrishna Seva Sangha

Ramakrishna Seva Sangha is the brain child of a team of likeminded philanthropists who are devotees of Shree Ramakrishna Ashrama Yadavagiri, Mysuru. Being ardent followers of the HolyTrio Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa, Matha Sharada Devi and Sri Swamy Vivekananda, their hearts went out to alleviate the miseries of the underprivileged and the down trodden.
